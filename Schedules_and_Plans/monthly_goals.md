# Monthly Goals  
*A structured plan to stay focused, consistent, and progressing every month.*

---

## **Purpose**
This document outlines monthly goals to help maintain momentum in cybersecurity learning, hands‑on practice, and career preparation.  
It keeps progress intentional, measurable, and aligned with long‑term objectives.

---

# **Monthly Focus Areas**

## **1. Learning & Coursework**
- Complete one major section of the Google Cybersecurity Certificate  
- Review and update notes in the Google_Certificate_Notes folder  
- Revisit any modules that need reinforcement  

---

## **2. Hands‑On Labs**
- Complete 8–12 TryHackMe rooms  
- Focus on Blue Team, SOC, Linux, and Windows fundamentals  
- Document findings in the TryHackMe-Labs folder  

---

## **3. Tools & Technical Skills**
- Practice with at least two SOC tools (Splunk, Wireshark, Zeek, Suricata)  
- Build or improve one Python automation script  
- Add updates to the Python-Scripts folder  

---

## **4. Projects & Portfolio**
- Complete or expand one cybersecurity project  
- Update project README files with findings and screenshots  
- Commit changes to GitHub weekly  

---

## **5. Career Preparation**
- Update resume with new skills or projects  
- Write or refine one cover letter template  
- Practice 10–15 interview questions  
- Add improvements to the Career-Prep folder  

---

## **6. Personal Development**
- Read one cybersecurity article or blog per week  
- Watch one security‑related video or talk  
- Reflect on progress and adjust goals as needed  

---

# **Monthly Review Checklist**
At the end of each month, review:

- What did I complete?  
- What needs more practice?  
- What should I focus on next month?  
- What new skills did I gain?  
- What projects or tools improved the most?  

---

# **Notes**
Use this space to track ideas, reminders, or adjustments for next month.
